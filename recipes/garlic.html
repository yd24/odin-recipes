Garlic Chicken

Description

A delicious chicken dish perfumed with amazing garlic flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb chicken breast
  • 4 medium cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and slice garlic into thin discs.
  2. Heat olive oil over low heat and lightly fry garlic until bubbling.
  3. Remove garlic and let drain on paper towels.
  4. Add salt and pepper to oil. Increase heat to medium.
  5. Fry chicken until golden brown. Add garlic back in and toss to mix.
  6. Drain chicken and garlic on paper towels, then serve while still warm.
